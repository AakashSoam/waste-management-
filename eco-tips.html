Eco-Friendly Tips

Reduce Single-Use Plastics

Opt for reusable water bottles, bags, and containers to minimize single-use plastics in your daily life.

Compost Kitchen Scraps

Create nutrient-rich compost from fruit and vegetable scraps to enrich your garden soil.

Conserve Water

Turn off taps while brushing your teeth, and fix leaks promptly to reduce water waste.

Switch to LED Bulbs

LED bulbs are more energy-efficient and last longer, saving electricity and reducing waste.
